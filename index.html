Lint buildup can restrict airflow, raise heat levels, and increase fire risk. Cleaning the vent helps support safer operation and better performance.

Can a clogged vent affect drying time?

Yes. Restricted airflow often means your dryer has to work harder and longer, which can lead to slow cycles and wasted energy.
